About me

My name is Tekla and I’m a dancer of Argentine Tango.

Since 2016 I have been teaching and performing in over 16 countries around the World. Also, trained in Georgian Folk Dance since the age of 5 and in Classical (Western Art) Music since the age of 6.

Since 2012, I’m as creator and choreographer of the first “Students’ Tango Festival and Championship” in Georgian universities, as well as the author of the first Students’ theater tango performance “Tango Mania” in Tbilisi (the capital of Georgia).

In 2017, I created TANGO ON HIGH HEELS - Women's Project in Tango, where I teach the importance of follower role in tango, improvisation techniques, my special author’s method and many more!

With my Partner Julio Saavedra, we regularly perform in various international festivals, marathons, scene shows and other bigger events worldwide.

I constantly work in 3 Academies: Tango Academy Tbilisi (Tbilisi, Georgia), Zlota Milonga Academy (Warsaw, Poland), Deseo Tango (Geneva, Switzerland).
